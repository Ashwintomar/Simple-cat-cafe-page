Coffee Gang

Across the Singapore River from the fashionable Arts House, this cat café isn't just about the kittens—they're purveyors of "Alfie de Meow," a custom cat-themed jewelry line (aka human catnip). Buy a brooch or two in honor of your best bud, with 10% of the proceeds going to CATSI, a local non-profit that serves the city's stray cats.
